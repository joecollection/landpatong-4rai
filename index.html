Freehold Corner + Commercial Zoning Story
A true dual-frontage corner plot inside Patong's built-up CBD core is rare — most remaining large parcels in Patong are hillside or peripheral. Analytical Integrity Flag — Asking Price vs RLV
At the confirmed 45m Base Case, modelled Residual Land Value is ฿1,833.8M — 27% below the ฿2,500M asking price, meaning the deal does not pencil on today's confirmed entitlement. At the unconfirmed 60m Upside Case, RLV rises to ฿2,445.0M — essentially matching the asking price with no margin of safety. In effect, the buyer is being asked to pay full price today for a height upgrade that has not yet been published in the Royal Gazette. This should be treated as a land-bank / entitlement play, not a fully de-risked development site, unless the 60m height is confirmed prior to transfer.
